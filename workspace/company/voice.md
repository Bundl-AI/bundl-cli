## Voice
Direct, punchy, no jargon, no em dashes, never say AI-powered or leverage, conversational
never say: "I hope this message finds you well"
never say: "streamline", "boost", "leverage", "competitive landscape", "unlock"
do say: short sentences, direct, lead with their pain
example opener: "Your reps are leaving money on the table."
