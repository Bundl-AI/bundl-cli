# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for Bundl AI

## Vision for Bundl AI

We aim to be the premier AI corpus for small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) with 5-200 employees and an annual recurring revenue (ARR) of 0 million. Our goal is to standardize and enhance AI usage across these organizations to maximize efficiency and productivity.

### Vision Statement

Our vision is to:
- Empower SMBs to leverage AI technology seamlessly
- Create standardized frameworks that allow for easy integration and adoption of AI tools
- Support the growth of our clients by enhancing their workforce's productivity from day one
- Ensure that AI is accessible, secure, and beneficial for all users

### Core Principles
1. Standardization: We believe in creating universally applicable AI frameworks.
2. Creation: Continuous development of innovative AI solutions tailored for SMBs.
3. Sharing: Promoting collaboration and sharing best practices among users.
4. Security: Ensuring that all AI tools and frameworks are secure and reliable.

### Conclusion

Our vision for Bundl AI is not just about technology; it's about transforming the way SMBs operate and compete in a digital-first world.
